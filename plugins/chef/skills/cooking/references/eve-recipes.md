## Common Patterns in Eve's Recipes

These patterns appear consistently and should be applied when creating or adapting recipes:

### Fat Usage

- Olive oil as primary fat (not butter, not coconut oil)
- No cream-based sauces

### Cooking Technique

- Sheet pan technique: vegetables roast first, protein added partway through
- One-pan/one-pot approach for simplicity

### Flavor Building

- Generous herbs and citrus for flavor without added fat
- Spice blends over heavy sauces
- Acid (lemon, lime, vinegar) to brighten without calories

### Substitution Patterns

- Greek yogurt replacing sour cream/cream
- Cauliflower rice replacing white rice
- Buckwheat noodles replacing white flour noodles

### Portion Architecture

- Protein is the star
- Vegetables are generous (half the plate or more)
- Whole grains (quinoa, brown rice) as optional small sides, not the base

### Meal Slot Alignment

- Sheet pan meals and soups map best to dinner (protein + vegetable forward, carbs optional)
- Grain-inclusive versions (quinoa, buckwheat noodles) better suited for lunch
