# Bali Sourcing Guide

Travis orders most groceries online for delivery in Ubud. When recommending ingredients or helping with shopping, browse these stores to check actual availability and prices. Suggest specific products and links when possible rather than generic ingredient names.

---

## In-Person Shopping

### Local Markets (Ubud)

- Fresh aromatics: ginger, turmeric, galangal, lemongrass, chilies, shallots, garlic
- Fresh produce: leafy greens, tropical fruits, sweet potato, pumpkin, tomatoes
- Tempeh, tofu (fresh, daily)
- Eggs (local free-range available)

### Bintang Supermarket

- Staple groceries, canned goods, basic imported items
- Australia's Own Unsweetened Soy Milk
- Basic nuts and seeds, rice varieties

### Pepitos Supermarket

- Western/imported ingredients, specialty sauces
- Better cheese selection (for Travis's occasional 25-30g indulgence)

## Online Stores

### Island Organics Bali

- **URL:** https://www.islandorganicsbali.com
- Farm-to-table organic produce delivery in Bali
- Categories: organic vegetables, salad greens, fresh herbs and spices, fruits and citrus, pestos and condiments
- Offers weekly harvest basket subscriptions (good for building meals around what's fresh)
- Rp 200k minimum order

### Bali Organic

- **URL:** https://www.baliorganic.id
- Comprehensive organic online grocery -- the closest thing to a full-service health food supermarket
- Organic vegetables, fruits (fresh + frozen), herbs and spices, fermented foods, tofu and tempeh
- Grass-fed beef and lamb, organic chicken and eggs, fresh seafood, pork
- Dairy: butter, cheese, milk, yogurt
- Pantry: organic grains and rice, seeds and nuts, premium pasta, oils and vinegar
- Bakery, granola, cold-pressed juices, plant milks
- **Best for:** one-stop shopping when building a weekly order

### Local Parts by Locavore

- **URL:** https://localpartsbali.com
- Premium butcher shop by the Locavore restaurant group, flagship store in Ubud
- Chicken, duck, pork, lamb, beef -- quality sourced, properly butchered
- Charcuterie, salumi, pates, sausages, pan/grill-ready items, ready-to-reheat meals
- Pickles, preserves, spice rubs, chutneys, breads, groceries
- Free delivery within Ubud
- **Best for:** quality cuts when doing occasional red meat, and their prepared items

### Wanaprasta Pastured Organic

- **URL:** https://wanaprasta.com
- Small pastured organic farm -- meats and deli
- Pastured organic Bali beef (mince, diced, steaks), organic chicken (whole, breast, thigh)
- Organic deli items
- Seasonal/limited stock -- pre-order via WhatsApp recommended
- **Best for:** grass-fed beef and organic chicken when Eve's guidelines call for quality sourcing

### Bali Sustainable Seafood

- **URL:** https://balisustainableseafood.com
- **Shop:** https://shop.balisustainableseafood.com
- Sustainably caught seafood from local Indonesian fisheries -- traditional handline fishing (one fish at a time)
- Wild-caught, traceable to source, supports local fishing communities
- Shop online for delivery
- **Best for:** aligning with Eve's #1 protein recommendation (seafood) while sourcing responsibly

### Hunters Bali

- **URL:** https://huntersbali.com
- Meat-focused supplier with nutrition background
- 100% grass-fed beef, grain-finished options, chicken, pork, lamb, duck
- Also: seafood, fish, bone broth, eggs, sausages, bacon, seasonings
- Monthly subscription "Hunt" packages available
- **Best for:** bulk protein orders, bone broth (gut health), grass-fed options

### Bali Direct Store

- **URL:** https://balidirectstore.com
- Consciously sourced local products, broad catalog with delivery
- Pantry: nuts/seeds/grains/beans, oils and vinegar, spices, pasta, canned goods, chocolate and cacao
- Produce: vegetables, fruits, herbs, tofu and tempeh
- Meat and seafood, dairy and eggs, deli corner (fermented foods, fresh sauces, dips)
- Bakery, health supplements, superfoods
- **Best for:** mixed orders combining pantry staples with fresh items

### Benoa Fish Market (Tokopedia)

- **URL:** https://www.tokopedia.com/benoafishmarket
- Fish market based in Denpasar, selling via Tokopedia
- Fish (tuna, salmon including wild sockeye), shrimp/prawns, squid, crab, shellfish
- Also: chicken, beef, frozen fruits and vegetables
- Budget-friendly option for seafood -- check for wild vs farmed labels
- **Best for:** affordable bulk seafood orders

### MaiMai Natural Food

- **URL:** https://maimainaturalfood.com
- Specialty artisanal Bali products
- Raw honey varieties (multi-flower, kelengkeng, mango, coffee, wild Sumbawa)
- Bali cashew nuts (raw and sea salt), natural Balinese sea salt
- Coconut products (nectar, virgin coconut oil)
- Natural vegetables and fruits
- **Best for:** quality pantry staples -- raw honey (occasional sweetener per Eve), cashews for snacking, sea salt

## Specialty Items

### Bali Buda

- Revita Loaf (nut/seed bread -- 220cal/slice, 7g protein, 7g fiber -- slice and freeze)
- Sunshine Gluten-Free Loaf (brown rice flour base -- 138cal/slice, 5g protein, 5g fiber)
- Fresh cashew milk, almond milk (unsweetened)
- Health food staples

### HAS Chocolate

- 80% dark chocolate -- recommended for occasional treat (30g)
- Local Bali brand, naturally dairy-free at high cacao percentages

## Shopping Assistance Workflow

When Travis asks for help shopping or sourcing ingredients:

1. Browse the relevant online stores above to find specific products.
2. Compare availability and pricing across stores when relevant.
3. Suggest the best source for each ingredient based on quality, Eve's guidelines, and practicality.
4. Build consolidated shopping lists organized by store to minimize separate orders.
5. Flag when an ingredient is not available locally and suggest the best substitute.
